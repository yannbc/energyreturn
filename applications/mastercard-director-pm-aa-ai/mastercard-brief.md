# Brief -- Mastercard, Director, Product Management, Advanced Analytics and AI

## Role summary

Mastercard's Services division is a rapidly growing non-payment-rails revenue engine comprising Cyber and Intelligence, Data and Services, Loyalty, and Advanced Analytics and AI. The Director, PM, Advanced Analytics and AI role sits within that business and owns product management for a specific AI/analytics product line sold to issuers, acquirers, merchants and governments. It is a scaled, enterprise-B2B product role inside a global financial services organisation, typically reporting to a VP of Product for Advanced Analytics.

## Top requirements (inferred from Mastercard Services Advanced Analytics / AI job family)

1. Senior enterprise product management with a measurable P&L or commercial impact
2. Deep technical fluency across classic ML (segmentation, propensity, fraud) and modern GenAI (LLMs, RAG, agentic workflows)
3. Experience shipping data and analytics products in regulated environments -- financial services, telco, government
4. Ability to work across product, engineering, data science, and commercial organisations
5. Stakeholder management up to SVP level across a global matrix

## Why the role exists now

Mastercard's strategic focus on Services as a growth engine has intensified as payments volumes mature. Mastercard has been publicly investing in generative AI, fraud and risk products, and merchant-facing analytics. The role almost certainly exists because the Advanced Analytics and AI business needs more senior product capacity to ship GenAI-enabled products -- whether in fraud, loyalty, or B2B data services -- and to avoid the classic large-org trap of brittle proofs of concept that never productise.
