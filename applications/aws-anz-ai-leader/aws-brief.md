# Brief -- AWS ANZ AI Leader, AWS Global Sales

## Role summary

AWS Global Sales is building out senior AI sales leadership for the ANZ region. The role sits inside AWS's go-to-market organisation, owning the commercial strategy and field execution for AI and Generative AI across Australia and New Zealand enterprise and public sector accounts. It is a field-facing leadership role, not a pure product or engineering role -- the person carries a number, coaches senior AI specialist sellers, partners with solutions architects on the largest bets (Bedrock, SageMaker, Q, Titan, Nova, Anthropic/Claude on Bedrock), and represents AWS to C-suites on AI strategy.

## Top requirements (inferred from AWS Global Sales "ANZ AI Leader" pattern)

1. Senior field leadership at scale -- managing AI-specialist sellers, solutions architects, and partner teams across a region
2. Credibility with CIO, CDO, CTO and CAIO buyers in enterprise and public sector
3. Deep understanding of AI/ML and GenAI production architectures -- not just demoware, but what enterprises actually need to ship safely
4. Track record of translating AI capability into commercial outcomes (pipeline, revenue, customer references)
5. Partner and ecosystem fluency -- model providers, SIs, and ISVs building on AWS

## Why the role exists now

AWS's AI strategy has shifted from "we have the infrastructure" to "we have the model layer" with Bedrock, Anthropic's Claude family on Bedrock, and proprietary Nova models. ANZ enterprise customers are moving from pilot to production on GenAI in 2026, and AWS needs a senior leader who can coach the region through complex deals where the decision criteria are changing fast. Historically AWS sells on unit economics and reliability; the AI category demands a different sales motion grounded in model choice, safety, and agentic production readiness. This role is the person who operationalises that shift in ANZ.
