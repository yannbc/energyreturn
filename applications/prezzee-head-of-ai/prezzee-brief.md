# Brief -- Prezzee, Head of AI

## Role summary

Prezzee is a Sydney-headquartered digital gift card platform operating across Australia, the UK, North America and New Zealand, with consumer, B2B gifting and enterprise reward programmes. The Head of AI role is almost certainly a net-new senior hire owning AI strategy and delivery across the product and platform -- personalisation, search and recommendations, fraud and risk, customer support automation, and back-office productivity. It reports into either the CTO or CPO and is expected to stand up an AI function from scratch or near-scratch.

## Top requirements (inferred from Prezzee context and typical Head of AI scope)

1. End-to-end AI leadership -- strategy, hiring, architecture, delivery
2. Shipped production AI in a customer-facing SaaS or consumer product, not just research or pilots
3. Fluency across classic ML (fraud, forecasting, segmentation) and GenAI (LLMs, RAG, agentic workflows)
4. Ability to operate across consumer and B2B product surfaces
5. Commercial judgement -- knowing which AI bets move revenue versus which are science projects

## Why the role exists now

Prezzee's category is under pressure from three directions: scaled incumbents adding AI-native personalisation, fraud and abuse economics getting worse as AI tools democratise attacks on gift card flows, and enterprise B2B buyers demanding personalised, high-deliverability campaigns. A Head of AI is the single point of accountability to turn those threats into advantages. The role likely also follows internal product reorganisation post the company's growth-stage funding rounds and international expansion.
