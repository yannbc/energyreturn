# Screen Cheatsheet

## Open (after pleasantries)
> "Before we get into me, can I ask — where is the current pressure point on the team you are hiring into? That helps me give you the most useful version of my background."

## 2-min background (if asked)
3 companies, 3 exits.
- Billcap → Tally Group, 8x. Co-founder/CEO.
- DC Power Co → Ion Group. CCTO.
- Pendula → Smart Communications. CPTO then Chief AI Officer.
- Now: Venture Partner, Black Nova VC.
- "Pendula is the closest match to what Relevance is building. I'll spare you the geek-out unless you want it."

## Why Relevance
> "Agents thesis is the next platform shift. Relevance is one of the few Australian companies playing seriously at that layer. I have already shipped the architectural pattern you are scaling. So this is a fit application, not a curiosity application."

## The seniority pivot (whenever it surfaces)
> "I am not trying to backdoor my way into a bigger role. I applied to a real role rather than send a generic 'love what you are doing' note. The role as posted is a senior IC. I have run a 20-person org through to exit. My ask for this conversation is: help me understand what kinds of senior conversations are worth having at Relevance right now. If something exists, I would value the founder chat. If it does not, I have learned something useful and so have you."

## If they say "the role is what it is"
> "Fair enough. I would still value the founder conversation framed as future-facing rather than this-role-fit. Founders at your stage often have unposted roles in their heads. And if there is no fit now, I would like to stay on the radar — I am doing venture partner work at Black Nova, so I am not in a hurry."

## Comp question deflection
> "Happy to talk numbers when we are both clearer on the role. I have been operating at CPTO/Chief AI Officer level. If we land on something senior, salary follows. If we land on something different, we figure out a structure that works."
- Floor: $250K AUD. Do NOT surface unprompted.

## Location
> "Sydney-based, happy to be in your office. Working rights AU/UK/EU/CA if relevant later."

## Culture probe (if they raise Glassdoor signals)
> "I have read mixed signals on culture. I am at a stage where I want speed and intensity over comfort. I have built two companies — I know what 'founders make all the decisions' feels like. The real question is whether I'd learn from these specific founders. That's what I want the next conversation to test."

## Close
> "If you take one thing from this call — I have built and exited an agentic workflow platform with the same architectural bet you are making. I would value the founder conversation, whatever level we end up talking about."

## Rules of engagement
- 50/50 air time. Do not over-talk.
- Buyer not seller. You have a Black Nova role. You are exploring.
- Do not recite the CV. They have it.
- Do not negotiate seniority with Aman. He cannot promise levels. Save it for founders.
- One question ready if they ask "anything else?"
