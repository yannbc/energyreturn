# Brief -- Nine, Head of AI Product

## Role summary

Nine is Australia's largest locally owned media company, operating free-to-air television, 9Now, Stan, Nine Publishing (Sydney Morning Herald, The Age, AFR), Domain, and 9Radio. The Head of AI Product role sits inside Nine's product and technology organisation and owns the AI product agenda across Nine's owned-and-operated surfaces: personalisation for streaming and news, content discovery, recommendation, advertising optimisation, generative tools for newsroom and audience operations, and increasingly the AI-driven editorial and rights questions facing modern publishers.

## Top requirements (inferred from Nine context and standard "Head of AI Product" scope)

1. Genuine Head of Product seniority -- vision, roadmap, stakeholder management across editorial, commercial and technology
2. Shipped AI product in a consumer-scale environment -- recommendation, personalisation, search, generative content tooling
3. Understanding of the publisher and broadcaster context -- audience measurement, rights, editorial standards, ad tech
4. LLM and GenAI fluency, including the risk and governance conversation
5. Leadership of a cross-functional AI product team (PMs, data scientists, designers, engineers)

## Why the role exists now

Media organisations globally are in an urgent reset on AI. The pressures are well-known: editorial quality under GenAI, content licensing and training-data disputes, audience discovery shifting from search to AI answers, ad inventory becoming more automated, and productivity expectations rising inside newsrooms. Nine specifically has signalled an operating-model push toward digital and streaming, and needs a dedicated senior Head of AI Product to own the agenda coherently across its Publishing, Broadcast and 9Now verticals rather than have it scattered across functions.
