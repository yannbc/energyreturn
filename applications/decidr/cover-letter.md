# Decidr — Vice President Product

**Applied:** April 2026

The reason this role caught my attention is that I've already built something closely related. At Pendula I was CPTO of a customer engagement orchestration platform where the core product challenge was exactly the one Decidr is tackling at a broader scale: how do you let AI agents operate autonomously within workflows while keeping governance, auditability, and human control intact?

We took a deterministic workflow engine and opened it up so non-technical users could drop AI agents into decision points. Intent classification, next-best-action, conversational agents, all running within governed, auditable workflows rather than end-to-end black boxes. Our enterprise clients (telcos, utilities, education companies) needed that hybrid model because they had large customer bases and low risk tolerance. The product challenge was sequencing when to use AI and when to keep things deterministic, which maps directly to what DecidrOS is doing across a broader set of business functions.

On the multi-product side: at Pendula I owned strategy across the workflow engine, AI layer, integrations ecosystem, and analytics products. At Billcap before that, I ran the full product lifecycle across analytics, customer engagement, and data platform products for five enterprise clients with distinct requirements. Both were early-stage, ambiguous environments where the product leader's job was to turn a broad company vision into a sequenced roadmap that actually shipped and drove commercial outcomes.

On the team side: I restructured Pendula's product and engineering org from IC-dependent silos into cross-functional squads, coaching leads on accountability for outcomes rather than output. That org scaled through to acquisition. At Billcap I built the entire capability from scratch as co-founder.
